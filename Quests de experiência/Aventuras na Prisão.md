   :   ![Drake Preso](../images/6/66/Drake_Preso.png)
2. Localize o Vulto espalhado no mapa.

   :   ![Drake Preso 1](../images/0/05/Drake_Preso_1.png)

     - (prt\_prison 167,81)
     - (prt\_prison 203,213)
     - (prt\_prison 161,224)
     - (prt\_prison 153,173)
     - (prt\_prison 205,138)
3. Entregue a [Carta do Prisioneiro](http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/6925/) ao Prisioneiro para receber sua recompensa.

   - 30% de chance:
     - ![708.png](http://static.divine-pride.net/images/items/item/708.png) [Menta](http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/708/)
     - ![703.png](http://static.divine-pride.net/images/items/item/703.png) [Hinalle](http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/703/)
     - ![709.png](http://static.divine-pride.net/images/items/item/709.png) [Izidor](http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/709/)
   - 10% de chance:
     - ![712.png](http://static.divine-pride.net/images/items/item/712.png) [Flor](http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/712/)
     - ![6511.png](http://static.divine-pride.net/images/items/item/6511.png) [Flor Bonita](http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/6511/)
4. Caso tenha dado certo, você receberá a sua recompensa.
