As missões abaixo são individuais e não são repetíveis, mas algumas dão acesso à missões diárias.

Todas elas podem ser iniciadas em [Rachel](../Lugares%20em%20Ragnar%C3%B6k/Rachel.md "Rachel") e não aumentam sua reputação na [Aldeia do Lobo Cinzento](../Lugares%20em%20Ragnar%C3%B6k/Aldeia%20do%20Lobo%20Cinzento.md "Aldeia do Lobo Cinzento").

## Ajudando as Crianças

1. (rachel 38,44) - Talk to Young Man.
2. (rachel 37,42) - Talk to Man.
3. (rachel 40,45) - Talk to Young Boy.
4. (rachel 36,45) - Talk to Young Girl.

Rewarded with 10 Amethyst Fragment

## O Preço do Trabalho

1. (rachel 181,51) - Talk to Old Man who asks you to talk with the Trader
2. (rachel 136,95) - Talk to the Trader. He tells you he dreamed of being an adventurer until he took an arrow to the knee.
3. (rachel 181,51) - Go back to the Old Man.

Rewarded with 10 Amethyst Fragment

## Coletando Opiniões

1. (rachel 155,236) - Talk to the High Priest. He asks you to talk to 6 citizens in Rachel that he will mark on your minimap.

After talking to 6 NPCs, return to the High Priest. Note that the 6 citizens are randomized.

Rewarded with 3 Amethyst Fragment and daily quest unlocked.

## Vendedor de Meia-Flor

1. (rachel 180,80) - Talk to the Kid who is interested in Half Flowers. He spotted one near a tree.
2. (rachel 70,135) - Talk to the Kid. He tells you that picking Half Flowers rely on a technique.
3. (rachel 72,133) - Pick the Half Flower near the tree using the following method: 20° Angle | 4 Leaves Left | 30ml of Water | 6g Secret Powder

Rewarded with 10 Amethyst Fragment and daily quest unlocked.
